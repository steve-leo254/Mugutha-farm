Why SECOL POULTRY is Kenya’s Trusted Name in Organic Farming

At SECOL POULTRY, we believe that healthy food starts with healthy farming. Our commitment to organic practices ensures that every egg and every chick is raised with care, free from harmful chemicals and antibiotics.

Over the years, we’ve helped hundreds of farmers and families access premium poultry products, expert advice, and ongoing support. Our award-winning team combines traditional wisdom with modern techniques to deliver the best results for our customers and the environment.

Whether you’re a smallholder farmer or a large-scale producer, SECOL POULTRY is your partner for sustainable growth and success. We are proud to be recognized for our quality, reliability, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Join the SECOL POULTRY family today and experience the difference that true organic farming can make for your health, your business, and your community.

Building on our legacy of excellence, SECOL POULTRY has expanded into fishpond farming, bringing the same dedication to quality and sustainability to aquaculture. Our modern fishponds are designed to produce fresh, healthy fish while protecting the environment and supporting local livelihoods. With every harvest, we provide nutritious options for families and new opportunities for farmers, making a positive impact on our community and the future of food in Kenya.
